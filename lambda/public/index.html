Helping Hand

Helping Hand is an Alexa app designed to help people going through a tough time. Inspired by the stories within Natasha Schull's Addiction By Design, this app was designed to help instead of hurt like the games explained in her book.

Within this app, you are able to ask Alexa to guide you through two different meditations, a long or short one, give you information regarding who to call or talk to in a crisis, distract you with a fun fact, or have her say encouraging messages to uplift you.
